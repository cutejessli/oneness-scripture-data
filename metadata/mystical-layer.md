# Mystical Translation Layer

The mystical translation layer is an interpretive reading. It does not replace the source-audited restored translation.

## Purpose

This layer gives a devotional and contemplative reading of the passage. It focuses on consciousness, embodiment, divine love, restoration, direct connection, and the movement from separation toward wholeness.

## Rules

1. Keep the source-audited restored translation as the primary textual layer.
2. Store the mystical layer separately or in a clearly labeled field.
3. Do not present mystical renderings as literal source translation.
4. Let the mystical layer illuminate the spiritual meaning of the passage.
5. Keep the voice clear, beautiful, and direct.
6. Do not erase difficult material; interpret it honestly.
7. Preserve verse structure so the app can align restored, familiar, notes, and mystical readings.

## App Display

Recommended toggles:

- Restored Translation
- Familiar Translation
- Word Notes
- Mystical Translation

The mystical translation should feel like a living contemplative reading, while the restored translation remains the textual anchor.
